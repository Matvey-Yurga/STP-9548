About the Game

Match Journey is a match-3 puzzle game that rewards every victory with a collectible surprise. But instead of coins or jewels, you’re building a visual collection of the world’s most beautiful places. Designed with calming audio, smooth controls, and carefully crafted levels, the game offers both relaxation and motivation. No rush, no stress—just rewarding gameplay and beautiful progress.

How to Play

  • Swipe to match three or more identical tiles.

  • Each successful combination earns puzzle pieces

  • Collect enough pieces to reveal a stunning world landmark.

  • Progress through difficulty levels that gently scale with your skills.

  • You can return to past puzzles or retry levels to improve your time.

  • Daily boosters and hints help when you're stuck.

Are there in-app purchases?

Are there in-app purchases?

Yes, the game includes optional in-app purchases. These may include booster packs, extra lives, or premium puzzle collections. However, the game is designed so that all levels and standard puzzles can be completed without spending money. We aim to keep the experience enjoyable and accessible for all users. Purchases simply help speed things up or unlock cosmetic features. You’ll never be forced to buy anything to finish a level.

What if I get stuck on a level?

What if I get stuck on a level?

If you're stuck, there are multiple ways to get help. You can use earned or purchased boosters like hammers and bombs. Watching an optional short ad may also offer you extra moves. Over time, your skill will naturally improve, so revisiting the level later can help. Some levels are tougher by design to encourage strategy and experimentation. And if frustration builds, remember there’s no time limit—take a break and return with fresh eyes.

Can I replay earlier levels or puzzles?

Can I replay earlier levels or puzzles?

Absolutely. You can revisit and replay any previously completed level at any time. This allows players to improve their scores, collect more puzzle pieces, or just enjoy relaxing matches. You can also replay finished jigsaw puzzles in your gallery for a zen-like experience. Some special puzzles offer variations each time they’re replayed. We believe players should enjoy every part of the game as often as they like.

Do I lose progress if I uninstall the game?

Do I lose progress if I uninstall the game?

As long as your game was connected to a Google Play account or Apple Game Center, your progress can be recovered. If you play without account linking, uninstalling the app may result in lost progress. We recommend connecting your account to cloud save for full backup. You can also manually back up your progress in settings if available. It’s fast, safe, and ensures your puzzles remain yours forever.

Can I play on multiple devices?

Can I play on multiple devices?

Yes, you can play Match Journey on more than one device. Simply link your game to your Google or Apple account and your progress will sync across all devices. Make sure to open the game online to complete the sync process. Your boosters, puzzle gallery, and level history will all remain intact. This feature is especially helpful if you switch phones or use both a tablet and a phone.

Are there ads in the game?

Are there ads in the game?

There are limited ads in Match Journey, and they’re fully optional. You can choose to watch ads in exchange for boosters or extra moves, but they are never required. We prioritize a smooth and relaxing player experience. No ads will interrupt your gameplay or appear in the middle of a level. Players who purchase any item from the store also receive an ad-free experience for several days.
